Main page / Bonuses

By making purchases, as well as certain actions on our website, you can earn bonus points for yourself. You can use these points to pay for purchases! Bonus points can be found in your Shopping Cart

How do I get them?

The first is registration. You are entitled to 100 points for registering on the site. It's not difficult to do this: just go to the tab My account

The second is shopping. If you are registered on the site, then after each purchase you will immediately receive cashback. Moreover, the more purchases you have, the more cashback!

And finally, the reviews. Just share your opinion in the product profile. For a simple comment – 100 points. For a review with a photo – 150 points. For a video review - 200 points*

* – in the review, you must specify your last name and first name, as well as the place where the order was made. You don't need to take off yourself – just perfume

Information about the current number of bonus points, as well as their history, can be found in your personal account: open the "My Account" tab and select the "Bonuses" item there
